Welcome

Dr. Lavanya Kunduru is a dedicated academician and researcher in the field of Physics. With over a decade of experience, she has served as an Assistant Professor at RGUKT University, Basara, and currently works as a Degree Lecturer at TSWRDC(W), Nizamabad. She has played key roles in academic administration, serving as the Head of the Department, Physics, and IQAC Convenor. Her research interests include materials science, high-pressure physics, and computational physics. She has published multiple Scopus-indexed papers and contributed to significant international conferences. Dr. Lavanya continues to guide students and contribute to the scientific community. This website showcases her professional journey, achievements, and research contributions.
